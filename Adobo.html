Adobo

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made with meat, usually chicken or pork, marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices. It is then simmered until tender and served with rice. The dish is known for its savory and tangy flavor, making it a favorite comfort food in the Philippines.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken or pork, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Total Time: 1 hour 15 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Add the meat and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add the marinated meat and cook until browned.
  3. Add the marinade to the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until the meat is tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice.
