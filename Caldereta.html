Caldereta

Caldereta is a rich and hearty Filipino stew made with beef, potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers in a tomato-based sauce. Often spiced with liver spread and cheese, this dish is a favorite during special occasions and family gatherings. It is best served with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef, cut into cubes
  • 2 medium potatoes, diced
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup liver spread
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour 50 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat oil and saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the beef and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add water to cover the beef and simmer until the meat is tender.
  5. Add the potatoes and carrots. Cook until tender.
  6. Stir in the liver spread and grated cheese. Mix well.
  7. Add the bell peppers and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice.
