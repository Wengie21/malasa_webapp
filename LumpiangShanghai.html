Lumpiang Shanghai

Lumpiang Shanghai

Lumpiang Shanghai is a popular Filipino spring roll filled with a mixture of ground pork, vegetables, and seasonings. These crispy and savory rolls are often served as appetizers or party food, paired with sweet and sour sauce for dipping. They are a staple in Filipino celebrations and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 carrot, finely grated
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Oil for frying

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 50 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine ground pork, carrot, onion, garlic, egg, breadcrumbs, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Place a small amount of the mixture on a spring roll wrapper and roll tightly, sealing the edges with water.
  3. Repeat until all the filling is used.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Fry the spring rolls in batches until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with sweet and sour sauce for dipping.
