Menudo

Menudo is a classic Filipino stew made with pork, liver, potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers in a rich tomato-based sauce. This hearty and flavorful dish is a staple in Filipino households and is often served during special occasions or family gatherings. It pairs perfectly with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly or shoulder, cut into cubes
  • 200g pork liver, diced
  • 2 medium potatoes, diced
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Total Time: 1 hour 20 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat oil and saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the pork and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the soy sauce and tomato sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the potatoes and carrots. Cook until tender.
  5. Add the pork liver and bell peppers. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
