Sinigang

Sinigang

Sinigang is a traditional Filipino soup or stew characterized by its sour and savory flavor. It is typically made with pork, shrimp, or fish, and flavored with tamarind, tomatoes, and various vegetables. This comforting dish is often served with steamed rice and is a staple in Filipino households.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly or ribs, cut into pieces
  • 1 packet tamarind soup mix
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cups water spinach (kangkong)
  • 1 cup radish, sliced
  • 1 cup eggplant, sliced
  • 2-3 green chili peppers
  • Salt and fish sauce to taste

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Total Time: 1 hour 20 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring water to a boil. Add the pork and simmer until tender.
  2. Add the tamarind soup mix, tomatoes, and onion. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add the radish, eggplant, and green chili peppers. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Season with salt and fish sauce to taste. Add the water spinach and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
