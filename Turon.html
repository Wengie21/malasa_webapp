Turon

Turon, also known as banana lumpia, is a popular Filipino snack made with ripe bananas and jackfruit wrapped in spring roll wrappers, then fried to golden perfection. This sweet and crispy treat is often coated with caramelized sugar, making it a favorite dessert or merienda (afternoon snack).

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe saba bananas, sliced lengthwise
  • 1 cup jackfruit strips
  • 12 spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • Oil for frying

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Instructions

  1. Place a slice of banana and a strip of jackfruit on a spring roll wrapper.
  2. Sprinkle a little brown sugar over the banana and jackfruit.
  3. Roll the wrapper tightly and seal the edges with water.
  4. Repeat until all the bananas and jackfruit are wrapped.
  5. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  6. Fry the rolls in batches until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Sprinkle additional brown sugar into the hot oil to caramelize the rolls.
  8. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  9. Serve warm as a snack or dessert.
